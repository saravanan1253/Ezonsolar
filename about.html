Creativity is Devotion

Invented World’s first

Unique structure for the slanted roof bifacial panels mounting

Established in 2015, the company is driven by innovation and technology, showcasing a unique structure for mounting slanted roof bifacial panels, marking the world's first.

Invented World’s first

Sunlight reflectors

Pioneered the world's first sunlight reflectors to enhance power generation from the underside of each bifacial panel, harnessing additional energy from albedo reflection.

Invented World’s first

Horizontal Dual-Axis Solar Tracker

Developed the industry's inaugural Horizontal Dual-Axis Solar Tracker, boosting power production by 100% compared to a similar Fixed-Tilt solar facility.

Invented World’s first

Cloud Radar System

Devised the groundbreaking Cloud Radar System, increasing power output by a minimum of 5% for solar power installations equipped with the Horizontal Dual Axis Solar Tracker, particularly during cloud cover obstructing sunlight.
