Benefits of Solar Tracker

  • Utilizing solar trackers exclusively can enhance solar photovoltaic power production by more than 45%.
  • Solar panels achieve optimal power output when sunlight strikes them perpendicularly.
  • Fixed-tilt solar structures are constrained in their energy generation capacity due to the sun's dynamic, multidirectional movement.
  • Incorporating a single-axis solar tracker with mono-facial modules could result in an additional average yearly power generation increase of about 16% compared to a similar fixed-tilt solar setup.

  • A single-axis solar tracker paired with bi-facial modules can potentially boost power generation by approximately 30% on average annually compared to an equivalent fixed-tilt solar power station.
  • Implementing a dual-axis solar tracker with mono-facial modules has the capability to increase power output by around 35% on average per year compared to a similar fixed-tilt solar array.
  • Deploying a dual-axis solar tracker with bi-facial modules may lead to an additional power generation increase of about 55% on average annually compared to an equivalent fixed-tilt solar installation.
  • Utilizing a dual-axis solar tracker with bi-facial modules in conjunction with sunlight reflectors for bi-facial panels could potentially double power generation, providing an average additional productivity of 100% annually over an equivalent fixed-tilt solar power plant.
  • Incorporating a dual-axis solar tracker with bi-facial modules, sunlight reflectors for bi-facial panels, and a cloud radar system has the potential to enhance power generation by approximately 5% on average annually compared to an equivalent fixed-tilt solar power facility.
