R&D

Ezon Solar leads the way in pioneering creativity, defining the future of sustainable energy solutions.

Exploration and Innovation

Research and Development stands as the cornerstone of our company, driving innovation and progress.
Our R&D division is led by a team of engineers with a strong passion for innovation. Dr. S.K. Radhakrishnan has spearheaded the creation of four groundbreaking inventions, currently pending patents:.

  • Horizontal Dual Axis Tracker(HDAT)
  • Bifacial Reflector
  • Cloud Radar System
  • Special mounting structure designed for installing bifacial panels on slanted roofs
  • Furthermore, multiple product developments are currently in progress within the company.

Ezon has established a comprehensive solar tracker testing facility in collaboration with a prominent Technical University, M/s. Kumaraguru College of Technology (KCT) in Coimbatore. This initiative stands as the first of its kind in India, showcasing our commitment to innovation and excellence.

Research and Development serves as the heart and soul of Ezon Energy Solutions. Each team member embodies motivation, drive, and energy, constantly seeking to enhance their knowledge, elevate technology, and boost productivity.

The team is dedicated to product development and innovation, ensuring that Ezon remains a leader in the field of Green Energy.

The fusion of theoretical expertise with hands-on practical experience gives Ezon a competitive advantage, positioning us at the forefront of the industry.

Quality Control:

Our solar plant designs prioritize performance, quality, and safety standards from the ground up.

A key feature of our plant designs is the incorporation of backup mechanisms, ensuring minimal interruptions and the lowest possible downtime in power generation systems.

We exercise meticulous care in selecting materials for our solar plants to withstand even the most extreme climatic conditions.

Ezon upholds stringent standards that surpass market requirements, reflecting our commitment to excellence in all aspects of our operations.
